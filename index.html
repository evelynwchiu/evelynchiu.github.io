Seated Storage Unit

The Seated Storage Unit is a sustainable, 100% recyclable ottoman with a flip-up backrest. We designed this ottoman to be 100% sustainable after conducting empathy fieldwork, which helped us realize the storage and seating issues that dorming students faced. We wanted to create a furniture item that was completely recyclable, as over 250,000 pounds of items from students in dorms enter the landfill at the end of a school year.

R&D, Rapid Prototyping, Project Management, Construction, 3D Modeling

Air Quality Sensor for Seneca Meadows Landfill

We created an air quality sensor with LoRaWAN connection capabilities to the Internet of Things, which has allowed for local nonprofit BluePrint Geneva to monitor the air quality around the Seneca Meadows Landfill. This is part of an initiative to allow members of the local community to speak up about the air quality problems that have arisen from the growth of the landfill. Our project earned a prize at the uSpark Living Labs Pitch Competition at Cornell!

R&D, Rapid Prototyping, Programming, LoRaWAN, Arduino, Circuits
